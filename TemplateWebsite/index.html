Our Mission

Our mission started out with one question. Is there a new outlet that does not pander to the Left or the Right while delivering quality news? We were tired to unaffordable housing prices, growing homeless populations, and bulky yet inefficent government programs. The Left and Right can focus on social issues but someone has to focus on improving the country and making every dollar invested into the community count. Our GOAL, is to improve the country for everyone. We can only do this through using knowledge to improve public policy without the need for financial gain. This is why we are recruiting engineers and economists to use their unique skills to design new ideas on old problems. Its time to reform our government that can work efficiently.
